# Bias & Fairness Playbooks

### From detecting bias to fixing it: a working guide for AI teams.

---

These playbooks close the gap between understanding 
fairness and putting it into practice.

These playbooks cover both sides: how to find bias in an AI system 
and how to correct it, at the data level, the model level, or the 
output level. Each section includes worked examples, code, and the 
reasoning behind each decision, not just the steps.

The scope is classical ML systems in regulated domains: credit, 
healthcare, hiring. Both internal models and third-party AI APIs 
are covered. Relevant obligations under the EU AI Act, GDPR, ECOA, 
and HIPAA are referenced throughout.

## Contents

| Playbook | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Fairness Assessment Playbook** | Context and history analysis · Fairness definition guide · Bias root cause analysis · Fairness metrics and intersectional evaluation |
| **Fairness Action Playbook** | Causal analysis · Pre-processing · In-processing · Post-processing · Validation framework · Organizational integration · 3rd-party AI API fairness |

Two case studies apply both playbooks end-to-end: a medical AI 
agent and a loan approval model.

## Where to start

Start with the Assessment Playbook. It tells you what kind of bias
you are dealing with and where it comes from. Then move to the
Action Playbook to correct it.

You do not need to work through everything. Most teams pick the
sections that match their current problem and come back to the
rest later.

## About This Work

AI fairness standards are still developing. This resource reflects 
current best practice, grounded in existing research, regulatory 
requirements, and real system examples.

The field will keep moving. Some recommendations here will be 
refined, and known gaps (LLM and generative AI fairness, in 
particular) are noted explicitly in scope notes throughout. Use 
this as a working reference, not a checklist.

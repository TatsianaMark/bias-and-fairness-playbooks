    Fairness Intervention Playbook

    Introduction

    AI systems across the bank support critical decisions — from loan approvals to fraud detection and customer risk profiling. However, recent internal reviews have revealed growing concerns about fairness. In particular, disparities in outcomes across gender and other demographic groups have highlighted a major organizational gap:

    While fairness assessments exist, fairness interventions do not.

    Different engineering teams currently respond to fairness issues using inconsistent and ad hoc solutions. This creates:

    • uneven mitigation approaches
    • duplicated effort
    • unclear accountability
    • risk of systemic bias persisting across systems

    To address this, we are introducing the Fairness Intervention Playbook — a standardized methodology that enables engineering teams to move from detecting bias to actively mitigating it.

    This playbook operationalizes fairness by transforming isolated tools into a coordinated intervention strategy that can be used across:

    • internally built AI systems
    • vendor APIs
    • legacy ML models

    Most importantly, it enables teams to act independently in common cases while still supporting escalation to fairness experts when needed.

    Why This Playbook Exists

    Historically, fairness work inside the organization has focused on:

    • measuring disparities
    • auditing model outcomes

    But measurement alone does not change outcomes.

    Engineering teams require:

    • clear intervention pathways
    • structured decision points
    • repeatable workflows
    • alignment between fairness goals and business impact

    Over time, several practical intervention tools were developed while supporting product teams:

    Existing Internal Toolkits

    • Causal Fairness Toolkit — identifies how protected attributes influence downstream variables like employment history or income
    • Pre-Processing Fairness Toolkit — improves representation in training data
    • In-Processing Fairness Toolkit — embeds fairness constraints directly into model training
    • Post-Processing Fairness Toolkit — adjusts prediction thresholds to improve outcome parity

    Individually, these tools helped in isolated cases. Collectively, they form the foundation of a scalable intervention methodology.

    From Toolkits to Methodology

    The Fairness Intervention Playbook brings these components together into a single structured workflow.

    Rather than applying fairness fixes reactively, the playbook enables teams to:

    1. Identify where bias enters the system
    2. Select the most effective intervention layer
    3. Apply coordinated mitigation strategies
    4. Validate fairness improvements without compromising system performance

    Multi-Stage Intervention Pipeline

    Each intervention layer plays a distinct role:

    Intervention Layer Purpose
    Causal Analysis Identifies structural sources of bias and intervention points
    Pre-Processing Improves fairness at the data level
    In-Processing Enforces fairness during model training
    Post-Processing Adjusts outputs to reduce disparities

    Each layer builds on the previous one:

    • Causal insights guide data interventions
    • Data interventions influence model training
    • Model training affects final outcomes

    Together, they enable fairness to be engineered — not just evaluated.

    What This Playbook Enables

    This playbook is designed to help teams:

    • Implement fairness interventions consistently
    • Understand trade-offs between fairness and performance
    • Address intersectional bias
    • Integrate mitigation steps into existing ML workflows
    • Validate improvements using measurable criteria

    It supports:

    • classification and regression systems
    • structured and unstructured data pipelines
    • internal and third-party AI tools

    And it can be applied across domains including:

    • lending
    • risk modeling
    • marketing personalization
    • customer analytics

    How to Use This Playbook

    This playbook is structured as a modular system of guides:

    Core Components

    • Causal Fairness Methodology
    • Pre-Processing Intervention Guide
    • In-Processing Intervention Guide
    • Post-Processing Intervention Guide

    Supporting Components

    • Workflow Integration Guide
    • Case Study
    • Validation Framework

    Engineering teams can:

    • apply individual components when needed
    • or follow the full workflow for high-risk systems

    Most use cases will not require fairness specialists — making fairness intervention operational rather than theoretical.
